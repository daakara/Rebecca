About Rebecca

Rebecca Nwose is a professional beauty and fashion photographer based in Lagos, specializing in creating stunning editorial portraits with artistic lighting. Her work captures the unique essence of her subjects, blending classic techniques with a modern, vibrant aesthetic.

With a keen eye for detail and a passion for storytelling, Rebecca has collaborated with numerous brands and publications, bringing their creative visions to life. She is available for projects worldwide.
