About Rebecca

Award-winning beauty and fashion photographer based in New York City, specializing in editorial portraits that celebrate natural beauty and artistic vision.

With over a decade of experience in the industry, I work with leading brands, magazines, and models to create compelling visual narratives that showcase elegance, confidence, and authentic beauty.

My approach combines technical expertise with creative storytelling, using dramatic lighting and innovative techniques to capture the essence of each subject.
