About Rebecca

Hi, I'm Rebecca, the photographer behind RNstudio, based in Lagos, Nigeria.
RNstudio is a portrait and lifestyle photography brand dedicated to creating clean, natural, and timeless images you'll always cherish.

I didn't start out planning to become a photographer, I simply wanted to learn a new hobby. But the moment I picked up a camera, I discovered something more: a way to preserve genuine moments and emotions that often go unnoticed. What began as curiosity quickly grew into passion, and that passion is what continues to shape RNstudio today.

At RNstudio, photography is meant to feel effortless and authentic. Whether it's a maternity session, family portrait, corporate headshot, or a simple personal portrait session, my priority is to help you feel comfortable and confident. I help you tell your story.

Each session is more than just a photoshoot; it's a collaboration to create images that reflect YOU beautifully, naturally, and timelessly.

I'd love to help you create memories that last a lifetime.
Call me 🤙🏽 and let's get started 😉
